About this Website

My name is Ryan Heslin. I recently graduated with a master’s in data science from American University. While I know Python, SQL, and bits of Lua, my main (and favorite) language is R. This blog is devoted to discussing R and its quirks, as well as data science generally. For more odds and ends, visit my GitHub.

Made with the Blogdown package, which implements the Hugo site generation template. The theme was forked from @jrutheiser/hugo-lithium-theme and modified by Yihui Xie. I consulted this book while creating this site.
