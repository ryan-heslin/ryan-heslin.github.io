Hello, world!

Published Sat, May 21, 2022 by Ryan Heslin

Welcome to my new blog! My name is Ryan Heslin, and I just graduated with a master’s degree in data science from American University. As I do for most of my side projects, I’ve defined goals for this one. Naturally, I’ve made them just clear enough that I can plausibly deny working on this thing is procrastination. serves goals I’ve defined just clearly that enough I can plausibly deny working on it is procrastination.

Those goals are:

  1. Engage with the wider R community.
  2. Find an outlet for the R esoterica I’ve picked up
  3. Learn a web framework, as a first step toward more sophisticated web design
  4. Practice writing, which I find satisfies me the same way coding does

I mostly plan to write about R and other data science topics, though I might broaden scope later on. If you’ve found your way here, I hope at least you’re entertained for a little while.
